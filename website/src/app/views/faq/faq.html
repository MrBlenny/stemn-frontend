Frequently Asked Questions

What does STEMN stand for? How do I say it?

STEMN stands for Science Technology Engineering Mathmatics Network. The MN at the end is pronounced in the same was as 'CONDEMN'

What is STEMN? Where am I?

Welcome to STEMN. Make yourself at home.

STEMN connects the international space community through projects. It’s a place for space-loving scientists, engineers and explorers to geek out about the latest out-of-this-world research. Literally.

What are projects?

Each project page is like a Wikipedia article. It can be as long or short as you like. But enough to give the community a good idea of your skill set and what you are working on.

Still stuck? Check out the recent projects for some inspiration.

What kind of projects can I post on STEMN?

Space is multidisciplinary, and it brings all the sciences together. So while the focus is on space projects, we welcome all projects in the STEM fields. Your STEMN profile is your own gallery, your platform, your voice. Tell us your story.

What about ITAR?

While the STEMN community is built around knowledge sharing, there are legal issues to be aware of. As a rule of thumb, only post information that you have permission to share. Your project page can include as much or as little detail as you like.

Wow! This is much prettier / useful than LinkedIn!

No one likes to use LinkedIn. We use it because we have to. Put a link of your STEMN page on your LinkedIn. Share your STEMN page with employers, and stand out from that dusty pile of CVs.
